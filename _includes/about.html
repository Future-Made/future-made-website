streamlining goodness with creative technologies

This is a "kept-open" call for all individuals and communities on the planet; who are into ideation, design, research, and development activities on observing the civic engagements and impact of demand-side-initiated hence promoted future scenarios (aka. demand-augmentation) and creating opportunities to extend the Freedom of Expression with Expression of Demand in several fields.

Future, Made is an independent Art & Technology collective, hoping to be creative on what it does + how and why it exists. Opted for making a positive impact via progressively conducting a set of experimental projects in the socio-economical and political aspects of a multi-lateral, inclusive and globally designable future; while fostering commons-making and skills-making, around new socio-economical value chains with humanitarian values in a grasroots fashion.

Future is observable, when there is a demand, intention and consent bases exist for efficient, effective and fair access to distribution, discovery and use of resources. Otherwise, there will be an ongoing need for collecting real-time data around "impressions", rather than incentivising through "organic intentions".
We should be ready for a future, where all ads are blocked because of the known and being experienced/forced, bad practices. Our work-in-progress projects have also partially inspired from Community Supported Agriculture, where a future-oriented clarity is given to farmers by the consumers, including potential voluntary and educational activities.

All concepts and projects are being developed to further increase the accessibility, sustainability of the Arts, Culture and Knowlegde, opening new channels for expressing local and global problems via causes included to improve the ability of people to organize and demand changes in an non-anthropocentric manner, with an initial dedication on fostering fair, safe, localized, decentralize-able art events of the future, by design.

Hence, efforts started with improving the, deterministic and high risk-taking ticketing/planning methodologies. Via creating future-oriented, temporary and/or long-lasting communities around the arts, culture and values. Hoping to enable generation of multi-lateral micro-incentives between potential participants.
