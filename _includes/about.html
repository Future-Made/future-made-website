About the Project(s)

Future, Made. is an independent Art & Technology collective in a think-tank form; progressively conducting collaborative ideation, design, research, and development activities to better observe the impact of demand-side-initiated future scenarios (aka. demand-augmentation) in several fields.

Making performances demandable with an approach that incentivices audience to declare their demand via fair, inclusive and participatory mechanisms.

Efforts started with improving the, deterministic and risk-taking performances ticketing/planning methodologies; and creating relationships through multi-lateral beneficiaries between participants. .

All concepts, projects and ideas will be developed to further increase the accessibility, sustainability of the Arts, Culture and Knowlegde, along with the ability of human-kind in an non-anthropocentric manner to sustain and with a dedication around foster green, safe, localized, decentralize-able and deferrable art events of the future, by design.
