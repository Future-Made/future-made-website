About the Umbrella Project

Future, Made. is an independent Art & Technology collective in a think-tank form; conducting ideation, design, research, and development activities to better observe the impact of demand-side-initiated future scenarios in several fields.

Those projects will have their own goals however, there is common concept planned to implement in each:
Demand-Augmentation.

Efforts have started with for improving the existing, deterministic and risk-taking art performances/exhibitions ticketing/planning methodologies.

Making performances demandable with an approach that incentivices audience to declare their demand with fair, inclusive and decentralized participation.
